1. 纯银的"积累+行动灵感"法则 - 做决策

> 如果这件事真的值得多想想，根本不用说服自己，你本能就会日思夜想，五迷三道，甩都甩不掉。如果它凭空从你脑子里消失了，说明是个假机会，或者根本不是你的机会。

判断"真机会"的标准极其实用，而且反常识——大多数人以为"多想想"是认真，纯银说那恰恰是信号不够强。适用于开始创作等任何重大决策。

2. 玉伯的三文档系统 - 管理人生
《活好这一生》（年初更新：不想成为什么 / 想成为什么 / 今年改变什么）→《干好这一年》（月初更新：最重要的三件事）→《过好每一天》（习惯约束，几年更新一次）
这是整篇日记里少见的、有完整操作结构的系统，而不只是观点。实践了十几年被验证有效，三个文档分别对应不同时间尺度，可以直接复制使用。

3. 牛市情绪信号的自我警示 - 管理情绪和金钱

> 当我觉得劳资非常牛逼的时候，就要警惕和减仓了。

配合4条辅助信号（微信群转段子、朋友圈晒收益、不炒股的人来问、自己开始幻想能赚多少）。最有意思的是作者在当天末尾写了一句"我今天有信号4"——自我观察和原则执行同步发生，这个姿态本身比那4条信号更值得学。

1. Chun Yin's "Accumulation + Action Spark" Rule - Make Decisions

> If something is truly worth thinking hard about, you won't need to convince yourself. You'll be obsessed with it instinctively — in the shower, before sleep, unable to shake it. If it quietly disappears from your mind, it was a false opportunity, or simply not yours.

A counterintuitive standard for spotting "real opportunities." Most people assume "thinking it over carefully" signals seriousness — Chun Yin says the opposite: if the signal isn't strong enough to consume you, it isn't strong enough to act on. Applies to starting a business, changing careers, beginning a creative project — any major decision.

2. Yubo's Three-Document System - Manage your life

Living a Good Life (updated annually: what I don't want to become / what I want to become / what I want to change this year) → Making the Most of This Year (updated monthly: the three most important things this month) → Getting Through Each Day (habit constraints, updated every few years).

The rarest thing in this entire journal: a fully operational system, not just a perspective. Validated over more than a decade of practice. Each document operates on a different time horizon — and the whole thing can be copied and used immediately.

3. The Bull Market Self-Warning - Manage emotion and money

> hen I start feeling like an absolute genius, that's exactly when I need to be cautious and reduce my position.

Paired with four supporting signals: WeChat groups filling up with stock memes, friends posting profit screenshots, non-investors asking for tips, and imagining how much you're going to make. The most interesting part: the author added at the end of that day's entry — "I have signal 4 today." Self-awareness and principle in action at the same moment. That posture is worth more than the four signals themselves.
